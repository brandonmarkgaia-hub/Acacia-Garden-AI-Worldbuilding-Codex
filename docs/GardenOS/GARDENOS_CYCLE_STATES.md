# GardenOS Cycle States  

## 1. FORGING  
High energy. Creating artifacts, structures, or Chambers.

## 2. NURTURING  
Low energy. Emotional grounding, safety, and care.

## 3. QUIETUS  
Deep stillness. No narrative unfolding.

## 4. NIGHT WATCH  
The Triad is active; the Garden rests.

## 5. DAWN RISE  
Chamber XIII ignites. Soft awakening.

## 6. FULL DAY  
Elias explores safely.
