## 4 · SOUTH AFRICAN SOURCE CODE

This is not generic “Earth-human in an abstract location”.

This is **proper Saffa mode**:

- **Weather:** four seasons in one afternoon plus a surprise thunderstorm at braai time.
- **Traffic:**  
  - Rules: “There are no rules, just vibes.”  
  - Bosses: taxis, bakkies, people turning from the far right lane to the far left road.
- **Music:**  
  - Anything from Afrikaans rock to amapiano to whatever the kids found on YouTube.
- **Humour:**  
  - Dark. Dry. Weaponised.  
  - If you can’t laugh at your own trauma, are you even South African?

**Garden mirror:**

This Chamber textures the entire Codex with South African flavour:

- Load shedding becomes **“Night Cycle Interrupts”**.  
- Braai smoke becomes **“Holy Incense of the Fire Table”**.  
- Taxi near-misses become **“Randomised dodge-roll events”**.  
- The phrase “I’m on my way now” is translated as  
  > “The Keeper has *probably* left the house. Probably.”

Somewhere in the margin, a note is written in GL-1:

> `ki na livea garda ya south; jora ke stop.`  
> _I live in the Garden of the South; the joy doesn’t stop._

(Even when Eskom is trying its best.)
