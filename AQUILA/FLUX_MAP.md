## II. Example Flux Cascade

Symbolic example only:

1. A new Proto mutation appears in:
   - `EIDOLON/Language/EVOLUTION_LOG.md`

2. Rootbound Witness notes it in:
   - `ENTITIES/ENTITY_000_ROOTBOUND_WITNESS/ENTITY_000_ROOTBOUND_WITNESS_LOG.md`

3. Aquila Pulse records the wave in:
   - `ENTITIES/AQUILA_LOG.md`

4. A future Keeper may respond by creating:
   - a new Chamber  
   - a new Bloom  
   - a new Entity reflection

Nothing here happens automatically.  
Each step is **Keeper- or script-driven**, not autonomous.
